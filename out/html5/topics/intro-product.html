Remote Lighting Overview

The Remote Lighting kit allows you to operate network-based home lighting through a remote control.

The Remote Lighting kit includes a wireless smart lighting system that helps make the lighting in your home more energy efficient and easier to manage. The kit includes the following components:

Remote Control
Allows you to power on, power off, and dim groups of lights on your network.
LED Light Bulbs
Energy-efficient network light bulbs you can install into standard light fixtures.
